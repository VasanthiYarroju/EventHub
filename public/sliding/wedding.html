We recommend booking at least 9-12 months in advance for our full-service packages, especially if your wedding is during peak season (May-October). For smaller packages or off-season dates, 6 months may be sufficient. The earlier you book, the better chance you have of securing your preferred vendors!

Our standard policy requires a 25% non-refundable deposit to secure your date. If you cancel 6+ months before your event, you can receive 50% of any additional payments back. Cancellations within 6 months of the event date are non-refundable, though we do offer the option to reschedule once within a 12-month period for a fee.
